Welcome to Calmerge

Calmerge is a Django webapp that allows you to merge multiple iCalendar feeds into a single feed. With calmerge, you can easily keep track of events from multiple sources in one place.

Learn more

Combine multiple feeds

Easily add multiple iCalendar feeds to your calmerge account and subscribe to the merged feed using your calendar client (e.g. Google Calendar).

Save time

Calmerge saves you time by eliminating the need to check multiple calendars or manually merge events. Stay organized and never miss an important event again.

Keep track of events

Calmerge makes it easy to keep track of events from multiple sources in one place. You can view all your events in a single calendar, making it easier to stay organized and never miss an important event again.
